Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you typically work with? +
I work with small businesses, startups, e-commerce stores, healthcare organizations, agencies, and entrepreneurs — primarily companies that need remote data support without the overhead of a full-time hire.
How do you ensure data accuracy and confidentiality? +
Every deliverable goes through a multi-step quality review before submission. I maintain strict confidentiality for all client data and can sign NDAs on request. I also use secure file transfer methods for sensitive datasets.
What is your typical project turnaround time? +
Turnaround depends on project scope — simple data entry or cleaning tasks are often completed within 24–48 hours, while complex dashboards or reporting projects typically take 3–7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive work.
Do you offer revisions after delivery? +
Yes. All projects include revision rounds as specified in the package. I work with you until the deliverable fully meets your requirements — client satisfaction is the goal, not just task completion.
Can you work with my existing tools and software? +
Absolutely. I work with Excel, Power BI, SQL, various CRM platforms, Google Sheets, and most standard business tools. If you're using something specific, just let me know and we can discuss compatibility before starting.
